Company Mission

A community staple within the Ottawa Region, Coco’s Chocolatiers has been Canada's number one chocolate company since 1915. Our chocolatiers live, breathe and dream chocolate and work tirelessly each day to perfect our chocolates to ensure each box of hand-crafted delights meets our golden standard of perfection. We thank you for welcoming us into your homes and allowing us to take part in your most cherished of occasions. Together, one truffle at a time, we can truly create something sweet!
