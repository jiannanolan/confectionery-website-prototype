Our History

A community staple within the Ottawa Region, Coco’s Chocolatiers has been Canada's number one chocolate company since 1915. Our chocolatiers work tirelessly to ensure the treats on your table are up to the highest quality for every special occasion . We thank you for welcoming us into your homes and allowing us to share your most cherished moments. Together, one truffle at a time, we can truly create a sweeter world!
